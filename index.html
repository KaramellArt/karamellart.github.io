This is the KSC Group software home page. We are the creators of G-Scoutmate (Girl Scout), Scoutmate (Boy Scout), Cubmate (Cub Scout) and DJMate (Disc Jockey) software packages. The scouting packages are the finest troop management (and pack managment) computer software packages available. DJMate is an indespensible tool for disc jockeys. You can get information on all of our products as well as links to the ftp site that contain shareware versions of the programs.
