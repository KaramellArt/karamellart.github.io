About me

I graduated in Comics and Illustration from the Academy of fine arts in Bologna in 2019. I'm currently majoring in Graphic Design Multimedia at the Academy in Naples. I spend most of my free time working on personal projects and fanarts. Character and Story are the primary focus of my artwork. I aim to illustrate stories that will move the viewer to immerse themselves in the world I have created.

The character designs, the color and lighting I use to execute these stories are neatly chosen. I love to build characters and worlds that live, breathe, think, and feel.
